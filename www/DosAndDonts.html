What to do when you see a suspicious object?

DON'TS

1.Do not touch or remove the object .

2.Do not open or puncture package.

3.Do not submerge packet into water.

4.Do not accept identification marks on its face value.

5.Do not pass metallic object over the package.

6.Do not direct flash light directly over the suspected object.

7.Do not cut the strings or wire.

8.Do not try to bring suspected device in security control room or police station. Always remove the people and not the bomb from scene.

9.Do not attempt to open the baggage by hand.

10.Do not use portable radio equipment or cell phones within 100 feet of the suspicious object.

DO'S

1.Do evacuate people immediately to a safe distance.

2.Open all windows & doors.

3.Place sand bags around the suspected object.

4.Inform Police immediately.

5.Inform fire brigade, Hospital & Ambulance also.

6.Assist the police in their operations and abide by their instructions.

7.If its a secluded spot or if you cant stay , remember where you saw the object. If you can, leave something behind to mark the location. Go back the same way you came. There might be other UXO in the area the safest route is from where you came.
